About Us: A Paradox Unfolds!

In the current timeline, excluding all alternate timelines, our company began on Friday, May 27th 2017. Due to the flexible nature of time, we've actually been in business forever! We love taking clients back in time, but be warned! Not everyone returns!

One day, our entrepreneurial future selves brought us a time machine and warned us to never go to the future. However, they told us to take people to the past for money so that we could build the financial basis to create the time machine in the first place!
